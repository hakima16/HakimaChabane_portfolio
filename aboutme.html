My Journey

My name is Hakima Chabane. I immigrated to the United States in 2016, and like many people starting over in a new country, I faced difficult situations, learned new systems, and rebuilt my life step by step. Along the way, I met people from many different backgrounds, cultures, and experiences, which shaped my perspective and strengthened my ability to connect with others.

I’m multilingual — fluent in French, Berber, Arabic, and English, and currently learning Spanish. Being able to communicate across languages has helped me understand people more deeply and work comfortably in diverse environments.

Education & Professional Background

I hold a Master’s degree in Mechanical Engineering (Energy Engineering) and later expanded my path into technology through Information Technology and Full‑Stack Development studies. This combination of analytical thinking and technical skills allows me to approach problems with both structure and creativity.

Professionally, I work in banking operations, where I support teller transactions, cash handling, compliance documentation, digital banking support, and client relationship management. My growing interest in business analysis and technology drives me to connect operational workflows with practical technical solutions, especially in areas like process improvement and digital transformation.

Who I Am Outside of Work

I’m a mom and a wife, and balancing family, work, and continuous learning has shaped my discipline, resilience, and motivation. My family inspires me to keep growing, improving, and building a better future.

I see growth as a lifelong adventure and I’m always exploring something new — whether it’s baking, hiking, dancing, or spending time with my family. I enjoy helping others be successful, sharing what I learn, and encouraging people around me to believe in their own potential.

Looking Ahead

My goal is to continue growing in roles that blend banking, business analysis, and technology. I’m especially interested in improving customer experiences, optimizing workflows, and contributing to digital solutions that make everyday work easier and more efficient.
